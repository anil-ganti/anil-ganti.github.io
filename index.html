I am a PhD student at Duke University in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. I work in the Sensor Array and Multipath Signal Processing lab under the mentorship of Dr. Jeffrey Krolik.

Previously I worked in various capacities in California's energy sector and the clean technology startup space. In this time I recognized the potential value in intelligent environmental management powered by ubiquitous and low-cost monitoring. I am now at Duke's PhD program to develop sensing hardware and algorithms for low-cost and resource-constrained platforms

Currently my research focuses on acoustic imaging, sensor development, and array processing on heterogeneous computing architectures.
